About Us

Coffee. ,a part of Coffee Day Limited, is India's
favourite hangout for coffee and conversations.
Popularly known as CCD, we strive to provide the
best experience to our guests.

Our coffees are sourced from thousands of small
coffee planter's, who made us who we are today and
we're glad to be a part of their lives. We opened our
cafe in 1997 at Chennai. It's an exciting journey
since then to becoming the largest organised retail
cafe chain in the country.

Our's have own the best coffee seller award for the years
of 2020 and 2022, and still proving to be the best coffee
seller in the country.
